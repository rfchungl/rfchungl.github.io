### Description
Here are a few examples of visualization I created when I was working as an intern at Copa Airlines. They were built from scratch and all the data extraction and ETS were performed by myself.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Platform used:
* Teradata
* Alteryx
* Tableau
* Excel

--------------------------------------------------------------------------
